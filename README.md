Project Overview
This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard built using 13,000+ e-commerce orders and 90+ products. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, customer trends, and revenue distribution to support data-driven business decisions.

Objectives
Analyze sales performance across different channels and years
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Identify top-performing products and customers
Track key KPIs like revenue, profit, quantity, and cost

Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Data Modeling
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Cleaning & Transformation

Key Features
KPI Cards:
Total Revenue: $154.57M
Total Quantity: 68K
Total Profit: $57.79M
Total Cost: $96.78M
Order Date Filter (Interactive slider)
Revenue by City (Donut Chart)
Revenue by Product Analysis
Sales by Channel (Distributor, Export, Wholesale)
Top Customers Insights
 Dynamic Filters (Customer & Product selection)

Insights
A few key customers contribute a large share of revenue
Certain products dominate overall sales performance
Revenue distribution varies significantly across cities
Wholesale channel generates the highest revenue
